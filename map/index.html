ABOUT

The NBA is full of an array of athletes that have continued to amaze fans for decades. And the ways in which they've done so has been done in different ways from can't miss shooting, high flying dunks, unbelievable passes and for some, lockdown defense. Which means they haven't found one formula and stuck with it...As the game and the player has changed the need for certain skills within certain players has swayed and we've taken a look into the importance of different skills among the players that have contributed in these teams success.

On the "Players" tab, you will find a dropdown and chart displaying common statistics for our chosen players. As to not overwhelm the browser, we are only providing data on the top 5 most commonly used stats in the NBA.

On the "Hometown" page, the user is able to compare where each player is from and the skillset a player may have in comparrison to other stars.

And finally, clicking on the "Skill" tab will lead you to an interactive map showing popular locations where a player with certain skills comes from. Updating this map will also update a bar chart that compares the players. Thanks for your interest!
