ABOUT

The NBA is full of an array of athletes that have continued to amaze fans for decades. One of the teams that has stood out with an exceptional history of players is the Los Angeles Lakers. With 17 championships under their belts the orginization has found new ways and talent to continue their string of successes. Which means they haven't found one formula and stuck with it...As the game and the player has changed the need for certain skills within certain players has swayed and we've taken a look into the importance of different skills among the players that have contributed in these teams success.

On the "Players" tab, you will find a dropdown and chart displaying common statistics for our chosen players. As to not overwhelm the browser, we are only providing data on the top 5 most commonly used stats in the NBA.

On the "Hometown" page, the user is able to compare where each player is from and the skillset a player may have in comparrison to his teammates.

And finally, clicking on the "Skill" tab will lead you to an interactive map showing popular locations where a player with certain skills comes from. Updating this map will also update a bar chart that compares the players. Thanks for your interest!
